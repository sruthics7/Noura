About Us

As a network of visionary Swedish angel investors, we are driven by a shared belief in the power of technology to reshape industries, combat climate challenges, and secure a healthier planet for generations to come.

Our mission is simple yet profound: To empower next-generation entrepreneurs whose innovations address critical environmental and climate issues.

By fostering deep relationships with founders and leveraging our collective expertise, we transform groundbreaking ideas into impactful businesses.

At Nuora Capital, we are dedicated to empowering visionary founders who dream big and possess the courage and resilience to transform their ideas into impactful realities

Our Mission

At Nuora Capital, we are dedicated to empowering visionary founders who dream big and possess the courage and resilience to transform their ideas into impactful realities.

Our focus is centered on FoodTech & AgTech innovations—sectors that hold the potential to address some of the most pressing challenges facing humanity and the planet. By fostering these transformative technologies, we aim to

Revolutionize Food Systems

Support innovations that redefine how food is produced, processed, and distributed, ensuring efficiency, accessibility, and inclusivity for a growing global population.

Reduce the Carbon Footprint of Food Production

Back technologies and practices that promote sustainable farming, minimize resource consumption, and reduce greenhouse gas emissions, creating a more climate- resilient future.

Tackle Critical Challenges in Food Security, Sustainability, and Environmental Health

Invest in solutions that address global hunger, mitigate food waste, and ensure the health of ecosystems, contributing to a healthier planet and society

What we do?

Our hands-on approach ensures that innovation transitions seamlessly from the lab to the market

Direct Investments

Our investment approach focuses on founders at the pre-seed and seed stages. We bring the capital, mentorship, and support needed to transform ambitious ideas into scalable, impactful businesses.

Capital Raising Support

Fundraising is a crucial yet challenging aspect of entrepreneurship. With years of experience and a robust network of investors, we help founders navigate the complexities of raising capital, ensuring their vision gets the funding it deserves.

Co-Founding Partnerships

We don’t just invest—we partner. By working closely with research institutions and universities, we co- found and spin out companies based on cutting-edge science and transformative technologies.

Our Investment Philosophy

Our approach is built on three foundational pillars that guide every investment decision we make

  • Transformative Technologies
  • Climate-Positive Solutions
  • Long-Term Sustainability

Transformative Technologies

Backing innovations that redefine possibilities in FoodTech.

Empowering visionary entrepreneurs and technologies that are transforming the future of food — from sustainable farming to smart distribution — we invest in ideas that redefine the boundaries of what’s possible in FoodTech

Climate-Positive Solutions

Supporting ventures that directly combat environmental challenges and drive sustainability.

We actively support and invest in ventures that are committed to tackling some of the most pressing environmental issues facing our planet today.

Long-Term Sustainability

Championing business models that balance profitability with positive planetary impact.

We champion forward-thinking ventures that prove profitability and sustainability can go hand-in-hand — promoting models that deliver economic returns while actively reducing environmental impact and fostering a regenerative future.
